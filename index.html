Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our product

What makes PlantFuel different from other protein supplements?

PlantFuel is made with 100% whole food ingredients like lentils and quinoa, containing no artificial additives or fillers. Our formula provides complete amino acid profile while being sustainably sourced.

How should I use PlantFuel?

Mix one scoop with water or your favorite milk alternative. For best results, consume within 30 minutes after your workout or as a meal replacement when needed.

Is PlantFuel suitable for vegans?

Absolutely! All our ingredients are plant-based and cruelty-free, making it perfect for vegans and vegetarians.

Where is PlantFuel produced?

Our products are manufactured in FDA-approved facilities in the United States, with ingredients sourced from sustainable farms worldwide.
